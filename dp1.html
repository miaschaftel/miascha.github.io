HAPPYLIVING

A platform for students in college to connect with other students and get advice on topics relating to their living experiences.

Timeline:

4 1/2 weeks, Sep - Oct 2021

Roles:

Digital Product Designer, Product Manager

Tools:

Figma, Procreate, Pivotal Tracker

Skills:

UX/UI Design, Graphic Design, Sketching, Empathy Mapping, User Personas, Wireframing, Prototyping, Usability Testing

Description:

This project was for a course I am currently enrolled in, ENTR 390 (Digital Production Design). This was the first major project of the class. I came up with the idea for HAPPYLIVING after seeing friends struggle to adjust to college life and living with a roommate. The product idea came naturally and felt like the best solution for college-aged people given the popularlity of social media products within the age group. I sketched out my ideas and developed them into wireframes using in Figma. I designed the high-fidelity prototype in Figma which is linked to the image above.
