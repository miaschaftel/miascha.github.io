ABOUT

I am a junior studying User Experience (UX) Design in the School of Information and on the Cappo Sales Track through the Ross Business School at the University of Michigan. Through my coursework I have become experienced with Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQLite, among others. This fall I am learning more about front-end programming for web design using the POUR design principles of accessible design. I am learning about digital product design using Sketch, InVision, Figma, and other industry-standard tools, as well as continuing to learn about data analysis and data manipulation using Python.


On campus I'm involved in organizations such as Michigan Business Women, the Ross Retail Club, the Ross Sales Club, and Alpha Phi International Fraternity.

I am passionate about creating products and experiences that are transformative and accessible for users. My past experiences in UX/UI Design, Sales Planning, and Product and Business Development provide me with a distinct set of strengths and skills which allow me to have a unique perspective in the product development and user experience design process.
